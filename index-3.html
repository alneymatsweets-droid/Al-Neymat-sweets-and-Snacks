Our Story

Serving Since 1949 — Jhang Sadar

Al-Neymat Sweets and Snacks has been a beloved name in Jhang Sadar since 1949. For over 75 years, we have carried forward the tradition of crafting authentic, organic snacks and sweets using the same time-honoured family recipes.

Every product is prepared with 100% organic ingredients — no artificial colours, no preservatives, no shortcuts. Just pure, wholesome flavour that has been trusted by families across Pakistan for generations.
